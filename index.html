About Me_

Who am I?

Hi, I’m Florence Azriel R. Migallos, but most of my friends call me Renz for short. As a student and a whole person, I consider myself a shy, introverted, and observant person; I find things around me amusing, which adds to the peak of my interest.

Outside of my studies, I have a passion for drawing. Sometimes, in my little bit of free time, I play online games or watch movies to keep me engaged more between these activities. Sometimes, I have trouble connecting with people due to my social anxiety, especially when trying to talk with my classmates since this college is still a new environment for me. Despite this challenge, I’m actively working on overcoming it to push past this limit.

Even at this moment, I’m still progressing as a 1st-year college student. I can’t wait for the upcoming opportunities I’m about to discover this year and in the upcoming school years.
